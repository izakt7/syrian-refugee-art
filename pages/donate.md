## Support Syrian Refugees

Your support can make a real difference in the lives of Syrian refugees. Here are trusted organizations working on the ground:

### Organizations Accepting Donations

**UNHCR (UN Refugee Agency)**
- [Donate to UNHCR](https://www.unhcr.org/donate/)
- Provides emergency shelter, food, healthcare, and education to Syrian refugees

**International Rescue Committee (IRC)**
- [Donate to IRC](https://www.rescue.org/donate)
- Provides emergency aid, healthcare, and education programs

**Save the Children**
- [Donate to Save the Children](https://www.savethechildren.org/donate)
- Focuses on children's education, protection, and healthcare

**Mercy Corps**
- [Donate to Mercy Corps](https://www.mercycorps.org/donate)
- Provides emergency aid and long-term development programs

### Volunteer Opportunities

**Local Refugee Resettlement Organizations**
- Contact your local refugee resettlement agency to volunteer
- Help with language tutoring, job training, or community integration

**Remote Volunteering**
- Many organizations need remote volunteers for translation, tutoring, or administrative support
- Check with organizations like [Refugee Support Network](https://www.refugeesupportnetwork.org/)

### How Your Support Helps

- **$50** can provide emergency food assistance for a family
- **$100** can provide school supplies for refugee children
- **$250** can provide emergency shelter and basic necessities
- **$500** can provide healthcare services for multiple families
