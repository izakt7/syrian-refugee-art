## Contact Your Representative

Use the pre-written email below to contact your representatives about humanitarian access and support for Syrian refugees.

### Pre-Written Email Template

**Subject:** Urgent: Support Humanitarian Access and Protection for Syrian Refugees

---

Dear [Representative/Senator Name],

I am writing to you as a concerned constituent about the ongoing Syrian refugee crisis and the urgent need for humanitarian access and protection.

Since 2011, over 13 million Syrians have been displaced—more than half of the country's pre-war population. While the conflict continues, millions of refugees remain in neighboring countries facing economic hardship, limited access to education, and uncertain futures. Many have been displaced for over a decade, and 50% of refugees are children who have known nothing but war and displacement.

I urge you to:

1. **Support increased humanitarian aid** for Syrian refugees in host countries
2. **Advocate for safe, legal pathways** for refugee resettlement and family reunification
3. **Ensure humanitarian access** to those still inside Syria who need assistance
4. **Protect refugee rights** and oppose policies that would force premature returns to unsafe conditions
5. **Support long-term solutions** including education, livelihood opportunities, and integration support

The Syrian refugee crisis is not just a regional issue—it is a global humanitarian responsibility. I believe our country has a moral obligation to support those who have been forced to flee their homes due to conflict and persecution.

I would appreciate your response regarding your position on these critical issues and what actions you are taking to support Syrian refugees.

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]
[Your Address]
[Your Email]
[Your Phone Number]

---

### Customization Tips

- **Personalize it**: Add a personal story or connection if you have one
- **Be specific**: Mention specific legislation or policies if relevant
- **Follow up**: If you don't receive a response, follow up after 2-3 weeks
- **Share**: Encourage others to send similar messages
