### Key Statistics Cited

#### Total Displacement (2024)
- **13.4 million Syrians displaced**
  - 6.1 million refugees and asylum-seekers
  - 7.4 million internally displaced persons
- **Source:** UNHCR Global Trends 2024, USA for UNHCR

#### Refugee Populations by Host Country (2024)
- **Turkey:** 3.6 million refugees
- **Lebanon:** 1.5 million refugees
- **Jordan:** 1.3 million refugees
- **Germany:** 800,000+ refugees
- **Iraq:** 250,000 refugees
- **Source:** UNHCR, USA for UNHCR

#### Demographics
- **50% are children:** Over 3 million child refugees
- **90% live below poverty line** in host countries
- **1.5 million children** born as refugees since 2011
- **2.5 million children** out of school
- **Source:** UNHCR, UNICEF

#### Conflict Statistics
- **580,000+ estimated deaths** (as of 2022)
- **13+ years** of conflict (began 2011)
- **Source:** Various humanitarian organizations, Wikipedia
